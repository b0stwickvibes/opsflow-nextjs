## About

Notion is an all-in-one workspace that brings notes, projects, and databases into a single flexible surface. Teams use it to track work, share context, and automate simple processes.

## Requirements

- Modern browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
- Optional desktop/mobile apps
- Recommended: workspace admins enabled

## Getting Started

1. Create or join a workspace.
2. Add a page and choose a database template.
3. Connect Zippay via an automation or export to keep transactions in sync.
