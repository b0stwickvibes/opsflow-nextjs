## About

Use no-code triggers and actions to connect Zippay with accounting, HR, and analytics tools.

## Requirements

Zapier account with access to Webhooks or the Zippay app.

## Getting Started

Create a Zap that triggers on “Bill approved” and posts a message or writes a row in your data warehouse.
