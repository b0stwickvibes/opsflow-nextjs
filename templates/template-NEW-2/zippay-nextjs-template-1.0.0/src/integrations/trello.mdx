## About

Keep invoice reviews and accrual tasks visible as Trello cards with due dates and owners.

## Requirements

A Trello workspace and a board for finance operations.

## Getting Started

Create lists for _To Review_, _In Progress_, and _Done_. Sync Zippay events to create/move cards automatically.
